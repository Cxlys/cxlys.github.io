About me

My name is Daniel Casley, a student at Exeter College pursuing a career in eSports and Web Development. I graduated from Exeter College A-Levels with an A in Computer Science and Maths, while also acting as the student representative for my class.

I have spent a large portion of my life being part of the eSports industry, competing in the ranked modes of many different genres of game - for example Shooters, MOBAs and Fighting Games - and being an avid viewer of each game over the years.

My passion for eSports has led me into wanting to be a part of the rapidly growing industry that I've loved since a young age and so with this portfolio I hope to show that I have the skills you need.

My personal favourite projects

EduQuiz

A website project that started as a college project using ASP.NET, Blazor and HTML/CSS elements to create an student-led educational website intended to be a mix between Kahoot and Quizlet, where students could submit questions for other students and answer them for points.

External Output

A talkshow developed by me and a group of others on the same course at my college. Intended to be an educational show about the happenings of our college esports league while also including elements of comedy. To develop this show, we used high spec equipment - including tools such as mixers and lighting panels - located within Exeter College's own TV studio and recorded it to be edited and released on our college YouTube account.
