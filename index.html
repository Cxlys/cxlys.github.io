About me

My name is Daniel Casley, a student at the University of Exeter pursuing a career in software engineering. I graduated from Exeter College A-Levels with an A in Computer Science and Maths, and a D* in Esports - while also acting as the student representative for my class.

I have spent a large portion of my life being interested in computers, and have never stopped learning. I have skills in HTML/CSS, C# and Python, and my passion for games also lead me into hobbyist game development using the Unity Engine, where I have previously participated in challenges such as the GMTK Game Jam 2021.

My passion for computing has led me into wanting to be a part of the rapidly growing industry that I've loved since a young age and so with this portfolio I hope to show that I have the skills you need.

My personal favourite projects

EduQuiz

A website project that started as a college project using ASP.NET, Blazor and HTML/CSS elements to create an student-led educational website intended to be a mix between Kahoot and Quizlet, where students could submit questions for other students and answer them for points.

Interdimensional Jigsaw

A game I made as part of the GMTK Game Jam of 2021. I produced all the code and graphics and learned how to use UI elements over a span of just 3 days, and released it to Itch.io on the final day, 3 hours before deadline. This project was extremely challenging as I had never released a full game before, but it was extremely fun and I've since been on the lookout for communities in my area, and for the opportunity to join in again.

Tech South West Esports Festival 2022

I volunteered to work as a stream technician for the TSW Esports Festival - a festival with tens of massive corporations in the area going against eachother in Rocket League. I handled the technical side of OBS, graphics composition and scoreboard.

This website!
